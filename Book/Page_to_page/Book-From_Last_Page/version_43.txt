The rustling of leaves and the distant hoot of an owl were the only sounds to break the stillness of the night, and a chill wind stirred the branches overhead, sending a cascade of leaves swirling around us.

"We must press on," Elara finally spoke, her voice firm but tinged with a hint of unease. "We cannot let fear dictate our actions, not now."
